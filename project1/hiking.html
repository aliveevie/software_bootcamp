glacier experience is a small group experience that offers an easy glacier hike on Sólheimajökull with experienced glacier guide and is available all year a round. The tour offers a pick up from Reykjavik or meet a location at Sólheimajökull glacier. Add even more advanture to your stay in the iceland and come explore this amazing glacier experience with us

Sólheimajökull glacier is an outlet from the mighty Myrdalsjokull and is surrounded by some iceland's most grand volcanoes and during summer time, a glacier lagoon forms in front of the glacier only adding to the charm location. This popular tour is perfect way to get in real contact with the true reasons behind the name Ice-land!
